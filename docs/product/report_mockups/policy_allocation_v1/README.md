## Gated Or Deferred

- Policy-Level Attribution (Gated): Needs a policy-level calculated engine before showing allocation effects.
- Blended / All-In Attribution (Deferred): Deferred until separate policy allocation and manager mandate reports are understood first.
- Production Policy Allocation Report (Gated): Gated on real target allocations, approved mandates, and client-ready approval.
- Current-vs-Proposed Policy Allocation (Gated): Gated on a proposed allocation workflow and current/proposed comparison outputs.
- Timing Attribution (Unavailable): Unavailable because clean timing inputs and an approved method are absent.
